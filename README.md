  <h1>Phōs Privacy Policy</h1>
  <p class="date">Last updated: 1 July 2026</p>

  <p>Phōs (“the app”) is a companion app for Coptic Orthodox Christians. This policy
  explains how the app handles information. In short: it does not collect anything about you.</p>

  <h2>Information we collect</h2>
  <p>None. Phōs has no account or sign-in, and it does not collect, store, transmit, or
  share any personal information. It contains no advertising, no analytics, and no
  third-party tracking of any kind.</p>

  <h2>Information stored on your device</h2>
  <p>Your preferences and a local cache of prayers and calendar content are saved only on
  your own device so the app works offline. This information never leaves your device and is
  not accessible to us. Deleting the app removes it.</p>

  <h2>Third-party content</h2>
  <p>To show the daily readings, the saint of the day, and calendar information, the app may
  request publicly available liturgical content from external services (for example Katameros,
  Coptic calendar sources, and Wikimedia). These requests retrieve public content only and do
  not include any personal information about you. Any data those services collect is governed
  by their own privacy policies.</p>

  <h2>Children</h2>
  <p>Phōs is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone,
  including children.</p>

  <h2>Changes to this policy</h2>
  <p>If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.</p>
